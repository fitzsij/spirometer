Assembly Instructions

Step 1

Lay the parts out

Step 2

Screw Mouth-Piece to Inner Tube

Step 3

Place ball into inner tube and ensure flaps are ok

Step 4

Attach cap with spring to top to complete

Usage Instructions

Step 1: Relax and Breath Easy :)

Pick up the Spirometer upright and put the mouthpiece to your lips

Blow as hard as you can -- the ball will float higher depending on how hard your lungs work

What reading did you get? Write it down along with what time it is on paper or on our app

Take a look at your chart and follow the instructions you get
